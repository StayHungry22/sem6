Dum Aaloo Biryani | Biryani Recipe | Dum Biryani | Potato Dum Biryani

Aaloo dum biryani recipe cooked in hydrabadi style. Potato biryani is a flavorful and delicious dum cooked dish made with basmati rice, spices, potatoes and herbs. Most commonly biryani is made with either chicken or mutton and is a favorite among many. This aaloo biryani is amazingly delicious and will be loved if you are a vegetarian.

Firstly the small or baby potatoes are marinated in a spice mixed yoghurt which is the beginning of the dum aloo curry process. Later the same mixture is used for preparing the biryani gravy with the combination of pudina, coriander leaves into it.

Ingredients

- For Marination:

  • 10-12 Small Potatoes(Aaloo)
  • 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi)
  • Bunch Leaves of Coriander
  • 2-3 Slited Green Chillies
  • 1 Tbs Ginger-garlic Paste
  • 1 Tbs Red Chilli powder
  • 1 Tbs Salt
  • 1/2 Tbs Garam Masala
  • 1/2 Tbs Turmeric (Haldi)
  • 1/2 Tbs Jeera Powder
  • 1/2 Tbs Black Pepper (kalimirch powder)
  • 1/2 Tbs Kasoori Methi/li>

- For Biryani:

  • 2 Cup Basmati Rice
  • 2 Sliced Onions
  • 2-3 Tbs Oil
  • 1/2 Tbs Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Chopped Coriander
  • 1 Tbs Jeera
  • 2-3 Cloves (Laung)
  • 2-3 Black pepper
  • 1 Inch Cinnamon (Dalchini)
  • 1 Cardamom (Elaichi)
  • 1 Bay leaf (Tejpatta)
  • 1/4 Tbs pinch Hing (Asafetida)

Procedure

First Peel the Skin of Baby potatoes and deep fry them in hot oil. Fry until they cooked 80%. (Check using fork by poking potatoes). Now Add a cup of yogurt in bowl, add a tablespoon of giner-garlic paste, spices like garam Masala, red chili powder, turmeric, salt, pudina, and coriander leaves. Just give it a good Mix. And your marination is ready.
Add deep fried baby potatoes into marination and marinate it for an hour. Boil 5-6 Cup of water add salt, bay leaf into it. Add Basmati Rice into water cook for 5-10 minutes. Drain water from Rice and keep aside.Add oil in vessel, add 1 bay leaf, 2-3 peppercorns, 2-3 cloves, 1 Elaichi, pinch of Hing (Asafoetida), half piece of cinnamon. Once all spices are rosted , add sliced onion into it. Fry until it turns transparent pink in color. After that add copped tomatoes, stir continuously until it turns soft and mushy.
Add marinated mixture of potatoes, cook for a minutes and add semi-cooked basmati rice on top of marination .Add a spoon of ghee from top of rice you can add colored rice to make it more Attractive.(Optional). Garnish with pudina, coriander leaves. Finally your Dum Aaloo Biryani is ready to eat. Enjoy it with some raita or papad.
